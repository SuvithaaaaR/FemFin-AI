Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to payments and investment-related transactions made through FemFin AI.

2. Successful Transactions

Once a payment is successfully processed and confirmed by the payment gateway, it may not be automatically reversible unless required under law or explicitly approved under this policy.

3. Failed or Duplicate Transactions

  • If a payment fails but amount is debited, the gateway/bank reversal timelines apply.
  • Duplicate payments due to technical issues are eligible for review and refund.
  • Approved refunds are usually processed to the original payment method.

4. Cancellation

Users may request cancellation before payment confirmation. Once the payment is confirmed, cancellation is subject to transaction status, legal requirements, and platform rules.

5. Refund Request Window

Refund-related requests should be raised within 7 days of transaction date with transaction reference details.

6. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are generally processed within 7 to 10 business days, depending on payment partner and banking timelines.

7. Contact for Refunds

For refund or cancellation support, contact support@femfin.app with your payment ID and registered email.
